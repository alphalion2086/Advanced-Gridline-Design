# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** March 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **Daily Scheduler** ("the App") handles your information.

## Information We Collect

Daily Scheduler is designed to minimize data collection.

- We do not require account registration.
- We do not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.
- The App may process data you enter (for example, schedule entries) locally on your device.

## Local Storage

To provide core functionality, Daily Scheduler may store app data locally on your device (for example, using on-device storage). This data is not sent to us by default.

## Analytics, Diagnostics, and Platform Data

If you install the App through app stores or run builds/services provided by third-party platforms (for example, Apple App Store, Google Play, Expo, or similar providers), those providers may collect technical and diagnostic information under their own privacy policies.

We do not control third-party data practices.

## How We Use Information

Any information processed by the App is used only to:

- Provide app functionality
- Save your preferences and schedule data on your device
- Maintain app performance and reliability

## Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

We do not share your data with third parties except as required by law or when necessary for core platform operations (such as app distribution infrastructure).

## Data Retention

Data stored by the App remains on your device until you delete it, clear app storage, or uninstall the App.

## Children's Privacy

Daily Scheduler is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Your Choices

You can:

- Delete app data from your device settings
- Uninstall the App at any time

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the effective date above.
